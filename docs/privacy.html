Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Your Privacy Matters

RetireScenarios is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection and Storage

Local Storage Only

  • All your retirement scenarios, account information, and financial data are stored locally on your device.
  • We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial information on our servers.
  • You control your data.

No Account Required

  • RetireScenarios does not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect email addresses, names, or any personal information.

In‑App Purchases

Purchase Information

  • When you make an in‑app purchase, the transaction is processed by Apple.
  • We do not collect or store your payment information.
  • Apple’s privacy policy governs payment processing.

Purchase Status

  • Your purchase status is stored locally on your device and synced through your Apple ID.
  • We do not track individual purchases on our servers.

Third‑Party Services

Apple App Store

  • In‑app purchases are processed by Apple.
  • Apple’s Privacy Policy applies to all transactions.
  • For more information, visit apple.com/legal/privacy.

Data Security

  • All data is stored securely on your device using iOS standard security features.
  • We do not have access to your financial information or scenarios.
  • Data is backed up with your device if you use iCloud backup (controlled by iOS settings), or if you choose to make a manual backup it is stored where you designate.

Children’s Privacy

RetireScenarios is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the “Last updated” date.
